## Hi, I'm Samraat Samiksh Sen.
First-Year Computer Science Undergraduate | Frontend Developer | Creative Director

I am a developer with a penchant for weaving complex logic into highly interactive, cinematic web experiences. Having explored various facets of creativity—from writing and directing theatrical productions to graphic design and oration—I approach software engineering not simply as a technical discipline, but as a medium for digital storytelling.

Currently pursuing my BSc in Computer Science at St. Xavier's College (Autonomous), Kolkata, I specialize in architecting responsive, user-centric interfaces. I leverage modern frontend frameworks (React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS) alongside generative AI and procedural rendering (HTML5 Canvas, Web Audio API) to translate complex thematic visions into structured, aesthetic code.

Enthusiastic and a perfectionist in my work, I thrive on continuous learning—whether that means mastering a new algorithmic concept, optimizing a backend Python pipeline, or dialing in the perfect glassmorphism UI.

What I bring to the terminal:

Technical Rigor: Building robust SPA architectures and state-managed user flows.

Creative Aesthetics: A deep understanding of UI/UX design, visual hierarchy, and cinematic presentation.

Adaptable Leadership: Years of experience managing cross-functional teams, moderating debates, and directing collaborative projects under tight deadlines.
